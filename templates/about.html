What is M-saada

"Msaada" is a Swahili word that stands for providing aid. We assist organization through our revolutionary system, which provides micro-donation. We help in automating payment or donation platform for organization's across borders

How they help

We have seen the problem with Kenyan elections. It is therefore through this revolutionary design we want to create a platform to support the our new Kenyan leaders.
